WHAT IS NETFIN ?

NetFin is a "smart" digital platform connecting online applicants of financial products and services to financial service providers. It also includes a directory of “facilitators” - whose role is to support and assist applicants in their approach – as well as other service providers such as guarantors, insurance companies and other organizations, who are regularly part of the financial network ecosystem. NetFin responds perfectly to the current needs of the market, by increasing the visibility of providers and promoting the development of the business level of the latter, through a digital solution. It also strongly promotes financial inclusion, particularly that of rural populations, women and young people. The relevance of this tool is even more current and pressing today, due to the emergence and persistence of the Covid-19. This service is offered free of charge to users and the platform is usable on any type of device, including the form of an application for mobile telephony.

THE BENEFITS OFFERED BY NETFIN

NetFin is an innovative concept that serves as a catalyst for all and participates strongly and actively in the development of financial inclusion. It allows to:
facilitate access to the various financial products and services available on the market;
demystify and popularize information about this offer;
benefit from a single anchor point where supply and demand find each other, together with additional stakeholders improving the quality of the relationship between these two parties ;
determine the profile of applicants with ease and speed, before directing them to an offer that meets their needs, according to pre-established criteria;
promote competitive spirit and performance ;
participate in the development of all economic players ;
provide a platform of expression for direct and indirect stakeholders in these operations.

HOW DOES IT WORK ?

Whether you are an individual or a professional, private or group, if you want to access financial products or services, you just need to identify yourself by providing primary data on your profile and specifying your need. This information is processed by the platform that "qualifies" your application, in order to offer you potential financial institutions meeting your profile and your request. The financial organization you will choose then takes over and manages the relationship. At any stage of the relationship, you have the opportunity to be supported by financial expertise, by choosing one of the facilitators housed on the platform. Related offers (guarantees, insurance, and expertise) are also available, in the form of directories, similar to those of facilitators. A "financial education" component is offered to you, through training/information capsules and useful links that lead people to have more knowledge over information and to be able to better benefit from the financial system. It is also possible to track transactions from your profile on the platform and have their status in real time.
